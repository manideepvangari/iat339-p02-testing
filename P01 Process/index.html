about.

Ascend is a fitness clothing brand created for indivudals who would like to take their training to the peak. We are committed to create quality products sourced from sustainable and environmently friendly materials. All our products are ethically created in quality workplace environments. Our company is a thoughtful person who likes to remain stylish while supporting the environment through buying ethically sourced clothing. This person will help anybody in need, and appreciates feedback to improve themself in any way possible. Our company might be attractive to consumers due to its style, pricing, and the way products are created.

John is a 18 years old looking for clothes to workout in. He makes minimum wage as a waiter, and pays for most of his necessities such as food and rent. He is looking for affordable, comfortable, and stylish clothes to wear wear while working out. He looks in the market and see that the prices of clothing for the typical fitness brand (i.e. Gymshark, Lululemon) is way too expensive, and want an alternative that can satisfy his needs.

This company is a fictional brand created for IAT339: Web Design and Development, Spring 2023, taught by Peter Loo and TA'd by Kimia Aghaei. A link to the course webpage can be found here.

Fonts

This font is called Cambria, it looks elegant and minimalistic, which could be a good fit for the type of brand I'm looking to make.

This font is called Monospace, it takes looking minimalistic and simple to the extreme, which could also be a direction to take the brand in.

For the body text I'm using Cambria, and for navigation and paragraph headers I'm using Monospace.
